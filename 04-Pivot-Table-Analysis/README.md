# Bike Sales PivotTable Analysis

## Project Overview

This workbook demonstrates how Excel PivotTables can transform detailed Bike Sales transaction data into organized business summaries.

PivotTables allow large datasets to be grouped, filtered, calculated, and compared without changing the original information.

## Goal

The goal of this workbook was to summarize the Bike Sales dataset and identify meaningful patterns across customers, locations, products, revenue, cost, and profit.

## Workbook

[`Bike_Sales_Pivot_Table_Analysis.xlsx`](./Bike_Sales_Pivot_Table_Analysis.xlsx)

## Analysis Performed

The workbook uses PivotTables to explore areas such as:

- Total revenue by country
- Total profit by country
- Revenue by product category
- Profit by product or product subcategory
- Order quantity by customer age group
- Sales by customer gender
- Average unit price
- Total cost by region
- Product performance
- Customer-segment performance

## Business Questions Supported

The PivotTables can help answer questions such as:

- Which countries generate the most revenue?
- Which products are the most profitable?
- Which customer groups make the most purchases?
- Which product categories have the strongest performance?
- How do revenue, cost, and profit compare across markets?
- Which areas may require additional analysis?
